About Me

Hi, my name is Magdalena, and I'm a freelance graphic and web designer based in Coeur d'Alene, Idaho. I specialize in creating custom websites and graphics that help businesses stand out.

I love creating!

Whether it's designing a unique logo or building a responsive website, I bring creativity and passion to every project. As a freelancer, I offer personalized, one-on-one service to ensure that each design truly reflects your brand.

When I'm not coding or designing

you'll find me traveling, hiking, exploring photography, or trying out new restaurants in the city.

Awards

Discover the stories behind two standout achievements that showcase my creative journey.

The Festival at Sandpoint

Creating this poster was challenging for me since drawing isn’t my strong suit. I found inspiration in the Sandpoint area, where you can spot Mountain Goats. So, I made a poster featuring a goat playing guitar in the mountains. To my surprise, this poster won the 2024 Series Lineup Poster Artist competition.

Inlander - "Summer Camps Cover"

This design earned me first place in the Inlander magazine cover contest for Summer Camps 2024. The magazine printed almost 50,000 copies, specifically distributing them to people in the North West area, with a focus on Spokane and Coeur d'Alene.
